<h1>Panko</h1>
<p>
	Panko is a dedicated packet dissection and analysis tool with a modern and sane API for dissectors and plugins.
</p>
<p>
	Panko is primarily designed to ingest compressed or raw pcap or pcapng files produced from other tools.
</p>
